Our Story

ThirdAxis was founded on June 10, 2024 by a group of friends who shared a passion for 3D design. What began as a school project quickly evolved into a professional service after we received positive feedback on our early work.

Despite being students in 9th and 10th standard, we've worked with professional clients including a company associated with the mobile racing game Drive Zone Online. We've also successfully sold 3D assets through major marketplaces like CGTrader, TurboSquid, and Sketchfab.

Our mission is to prove that age is just a number when it comes to creativity and professionalism. We're constantly learning, improving our skills, and pushing the boundaries of what young creators can achieve in the digital design space.
