About ThirdAxis

ThirdAxis is a passionate student-led 3D modeling and digital design group based in Lucknow, India. Founded on June 10, 2024, our team consists of six talented students currently in 9th and 10th standard who have come together to create high-quality 3D assets.

Despite our young age, we've developed strong skills in 3D modeling, texturing, animation, rendering, and client communication. We create and sell digital 3D models through leading marketplaces like CGTrader, TurboSquid, and Sketchfab.

We also offer freelance services for small studios, creators, and developers looking for custom 3D work. Our mission is to combine our passion for digital creation with real-world experience while still in school.
